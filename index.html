About CAL 2026

The Crypto Asset Lab Conference 2026 explores the accelerating convergence between traditional finance (TradFi) and decentralized finance (DeFi), with a special emphasis on stablecoins and tokenised deposits. Experts from academia, industry, and policy will examine regulatory frameworks, technological infrastructure, risk management, and real-world adoption cases.

Call for Papers

We invite researchers, innovators, and practitioners to submit original contributions on topics including:

  • Stablecoin and tokenised deposit regulation
  • CBDCs and monetary policy interactions
  • Auditing and risk frameworks for token-based instruments
  • Cross-platform TradFi-DeFi interoperability
  • Payment systems and infrastructure integration

Deadline: 30 September 2025
