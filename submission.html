How to Submit

Submissions for CAL 2026 will open on 1 June 2025 and close on 1 December 2025.

Authors will be notified of acceptance by 15 January 2026.

To submit your paper, please email your manuscript (PDF or Word format) to:
papers@cal2026.org

Submissions should include:

  • Title and abstract (up to 300 words)
  • Full name(s) and affiliation(s) of the author(s)
  • Keywords (max 5)
  • Any applicable disclosures or declarations of interest
